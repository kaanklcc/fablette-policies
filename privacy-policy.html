Fablette uses Firebase Anonymous Authentication. This means:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal identification information.
  • A unique anonymous identifier (UID) is automatically generated when you first open the app.

Important: We do not collect or store any payment card details, billing addresses, or financial information. All payment processing is handled securely by the respective platform:

Android (Google Play)

Payments and subscriptions are processed entirely by Google Play Billing via RevenueCat. All payment and billing information is managed by Google according to Google's Privacy Policy.

iOS (App Store)

AI-GENERATED CONTENT DISCLOSURE

Important Information for Parents and Guardians

Fablette uses advanced artificial intelligence technology to create unique stories for children. Please read this disclosure carefully.

1. AI MODELS USED

Our app uses the following AI models:

  • OpenAI GPT-4: Story text generation and text-to-speech narration
  • Google Gemini 2.0: Story generation and image creation

2. HOW AI CONTENT IS GENERATED

When you create a story:

  1. You provide story parameters (theme, characters, location, topic)
  2. AI generates story text based on your inputs
  3. AI creates illustrations matching the story
  4. AI converts text to natural-sounding speech

Data Handling: Your story prompts are sent to AI services for content generation. These prompts are processed in real-time and are not used to train AI models. No personal information is included in the prompts sent to AI services.

3. CONTENT SAFETY

While we implement safety measures, please note:

  • AI-generated content is not pre-reviewed by humans.
  • Content may occasionally be unexpected or contextually inappropriate.
  • We use content filters and child-friendly AI settings, but they are not perfect.
  • Parental supervision is strongly recommended.

4. WHAT WE DO TO ENSURE SAFETY

  • Use child-friendly AI model configurations and system prompts
  • Apply content filtering on all generated text and images
  • Provide tools to report inappropriate content
  • Regularly review and update safety parameters
  • Monitor content generation patterns for safety issues

5. PARENTAL GUIDANCE

We recommend that parents and guardians:

  • Review stories before sharing with younger children
  • Monitor the content and prompts children are using
  • Discuss story themes and topics with children
  • Report any inappropriate content immediately
  • Use built-in device parental controls

6. REPORTING INAPPROPRIATE CONTENT

If you encounter inappropriate content:

  1. Do not share the story with others
  2. Delete the story from your library
  3. Contact us immediately at: fabletteai@gmail.com
  4. Include a description of the issue and, if possible, the story details

7. DATA USAGE IN AI GENERATION

  • Story prompts are sent to AI services (OpenAI, Google) for real-time content generation.
  • Generated content (text, images, audio) is stored on Firebase servers in your personal library.
  • We do not use your stories or prompts to train AI models.
  • No personally identifiable information is sent to AI services.
  • Your data is protected according to our Privacy Policy.

8. LIMITATIONS

  • AI cannot replace human creativity, judgment, or supervision.
  • Generated stories may contain factual inaccuracies.
  • Cultural and contextual appropriateness may vary.
  • Language quality and grammar may not always be perfect.
  • AI-generated images may not perfectly match story descriptions.
  • Content quality depends on the clarity and appropriateness of user inputs.

9. YOUR RIGHTS
